## üéØ Three Core Modes

### **Mode 1: Competitor Content Generator** üèÜ
**Use Case**: "I want to outrank this competitor article"

**Flow**:
1. Enter competitor URL(s) or keyword
2. System analyzes top-ranking content
3. Identifies SEO gaps and opportunities
4. **Generates NEW article** that:
   - Covers same topic comprehensively
   - Fills all SEO gaps found
   - Better structure and readability
   - Optimized for target keyword
   - Includes meta tags, headings, internal links

**Output**: Complete, publish-ready article that outperforms competitors

**Target Users**: 
- Content creators starting fresh
- Agencies creating client content
- Affiliates entering new niches

---

### **Mode 2: Self-Audit & One-Click Fixes** üîß
**Use Case**: "I have existing content that needs SEO improvement"

**Flow**:
1. Enter your article URL
2. System compares vs top 10 competitors
3. Identifies specific issues:
   - Missing keywords (with exact insertion points)
   - Thin content sections
   - Poor heading structure
   - Weak meta tags
   - Missing internal links
4. Shows preview of all changes
5. **Click "Apply Fixes"** ‚Üí Content automatically updated

**Output**: Your article, surgically improved with all SEO gaps filled

**Target Users**:
- Webmasters with existing content libraries
- SEO managers optimizing portfolios
- Affiliates refreshing old content

---

### **Mode 3: Dynamic Content Monitor** üîÑ
**Use Case**: "Keep my iGaming content automatically updated"

**Flow**:
1. Set up monitoring rules:
   - What to track (bonuses, odds, regulations)
   - Where to check (official sites, regulatory bodies)
   - Which pages to update
2. System automatically:
   - Scans sources daily/weekly
   - Detects changes (¬£20 bonus ‚Üí ¬£25)
   - Identifies affected pages
   - Generates updated content
3. Review changes or auto-apply
4. **Bulk update** across entire site

**Output**: Always-current content without manual checking

**Target Users**:
- iGaming affiliates (primary)
- Finance/crypto sites
- Any site with frequently changing data
